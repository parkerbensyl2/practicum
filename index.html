Case Study
Objective: To establish branding for a family owned honey company, including packaging, stationery, and social concepts. Research: As there was no current branding, the research for this project began with examining outside brands that the client enjoyed and successful point of sale systems. Process: The Golden Bear Honey Co. logo was designed with a clean, clear cut aesthetic in mind. The bold display type (Playfair Display) combined with a classic sans serif (Mr. Eaves) creates a clear identity for the brand. Close

who i am.

Hey there! I'm a graphic designer based in Kansas City, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Design at Missouri State University. Design, for me, is more than just a job. It's a way to express myself and make a meaningful impact. I believe in the power of design to evoke emotions, tell stories, and shape experiences. I pride myself in pouring my heart and soul into every project.
    When I'm not immersed in design projects, you'll often find me traveling to new and exciting places. Exploring the world fuels my creativity and provides me with inspiration from different cultures and landscapes. Istanbul, Egypt, and the Grand Canyon have been my favorite so far.
